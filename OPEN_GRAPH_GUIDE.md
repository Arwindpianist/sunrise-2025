## 🔧 Optimization Tips

### **Image Best Practices**
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630px (16:9 ratio)
- **File size**: Under 1MB (yours is perfect at ~295KB)
- **Format**: PNG for quality, JPG for smaller size
- **Content**: Include your logo and key message

### **Text Optimization**
- **Title**: Keep under 60 characters
- **Description**: Keep under 160 characters
- **Keywords**: Include relevant terms for SEO

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### **If Image Doesn't Show**
1. **Clear cache**: Social platforms cache images
2. **Wait 24 hours**: Some platforms take time to update
3. **Check URL**: Ensure the image URL is accessible
4. **Validate**: Use the testing tools above

### **Common Issues**
- **Wrong dimensions**: Ensure 1200x630px
- **File too large**: Compress if over 1MB
- **Wrong format**: Use PNG or JPG
- **Caching**: Wait for platforms to refresh
